About Supercell

Supercell is a mobile game developer based in Helsinki, Finalnd, with offices in San Francisco, Tokyo, Seout and Beijing.

Its games are availbale for tablets and smartphones using both Apple's iOS and Google's Android opertaing systems.

Since its founding in 2010, Supercell has brought four games to the market - Hay Day, Clash of Clans, Boom Beach and Clash Royale.
